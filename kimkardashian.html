Kim Kardashian

Kim Kardashian was born on October 21, 1980 to Kris and Robert Kardashian. Kim is currently married to Kanye West and they have three children. Some of her businesses include her app Kim Kardashian:Hollywood, her make up line KKW Beauty,and her numerous television appearances.

Kim receives a lot of hate for being "famous for being famous" and having no talent- but she has branded herself into a household name and now has a net worth of over $350 million.
